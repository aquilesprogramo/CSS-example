Super Mario Galaxy (2007)

Super Mario Galaxy first came out to the wii in 2007 and is the first major Mario game to do it. It was considered an absolute hit receiving praising reviews from every game reviewer. It was considered the apex mario game utilizing the same moves from 64 and sunshine but adding even more stuff into it such as different gravity and such. It is a must play not only to Mario fans but for every gamer.
