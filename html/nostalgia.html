Nostalgia

Nostalgia is described as a wistful or excessively sentimental yearning for return to or of some past period or irrecoverable condition.

Nostalgia is one of the strongest sensations that the human being can experience as well as being one of the most common. Everyone feels nostalgic throughout their lives and many people wish to return to the moment from which they felt nostalgia, to relive some forgotten emotion or to recover something lost.

A nostalgia trip is when you are reminded of a specific event which made an impact to you. Many people wish to experience a nostalgia trip and now for some of them it's possible.
